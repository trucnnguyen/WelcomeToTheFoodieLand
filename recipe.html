Easy Overnight Oats:

Ingredients

  • Rolled Oats(1 Cup)
  • Milk(1 cup any kind of milk)
  • Chia seed (1tbsp)
  • Yogurt

Instructions:

  1. Combine oats, chia seed, milk, and yogurt in a container.
  2. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  3. In the morning, add honey and fruits. Enjoy!

Lemongrass Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh
  • Garlic, red onion
  • Lemongrass
  • Fish Sauce
  • Sugar

Instructions:

  1. Mince the garlic, red onion, and lemongrass.
  2. Marinate the chicken thigh with minced ingredients.
  3. Grill the marinated chicken until cooked through.
  4. Season with fish sauce and sugar to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
