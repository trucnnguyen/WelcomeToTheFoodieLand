Easy Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • Rolled Oats (1 Cup)
  • Milk (1 cup any kind of milk)
  • Chia Seeds (1 tbsp)
  • Yogurt

Instructions

  1. Combine oats, chia seeds, milk, and yogurt in a container.
  2. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  3. In the morning, add honey and fruits. Enjoy!

Lemongrass Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Garlic, Red Onion
  • Lemongrass
  • Fish Sauce
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Mince the garlic, red onion, and lemongrass.
  2. Marinate the chicken thighs with the minced ingredients.
  3. Grill the marinated chicken until cooked through.
  4. Season with fish sauce and sugar to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Protein Oats with Banana Cake

Ingredients

  • Oats or Rolled Oats
  • Eggs
  • Banana
  • Yogurt
  • Milk or plant-based milk
  • Honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add honey for sweetness, if desired.
  3. Bake at 350F for 25 minutes

Summer Salad

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Red bell pepper
  • Feta cheese
  • Olives
  • Balsamic vinaigrette

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop all the vegetables.
  2. Toss them together with mixed greens in a large bowl.
  3. Sprinkle feta cheese and olives on top.
  4. Drizzle balsamic vinaigrette as dressing.
  5. Enjoy this refreshing salad!
